About

I'm curious about tinkering and modding. Oftentimes novelty draws me in — novelty as in "things that don't exist yet" rather than products. There's a boundary, or an edge, where things haven't been created hundreds of times. And then there's comfort in not needing to be better than those hundreds. Novel ideas combined with a wide array of skills. That's what AI doesn't have. It's distinctly human. I enjoy being in such a place.

I explore those ideas in my daily life. While the results can be rough and unfinished, they always fueled my autodidactic passion. I did it for myself. The result isn't a beautiful social media video section, but a continuous narrative of creating in a somewhat messy life.

This is a point of consolidation for me — to finally fuse my personal skills and professional career path.
